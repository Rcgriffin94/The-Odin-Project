Ultimate Loaded Nachos

The best nachos!

This is “nacho” ordinary plate. This heaping pile of nachos is loaded with tri-tip, chicken, and sausage, then topped with melted cheese and all the classic fixins

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of tortilla chips
  • .5 cup of fresh salsa
  • 1 poinds of kielbasa sausage, cooked and sliced
  • 1 cup of shredded cooked chicken breast
  • 1 pound of tri-tip, cooked and cubed
  • .25 cup of scallions, sliced
  • 1 small jar jalapeños, sliced
  • .25 cup of black olives, sliced
  • 1.5 cup of cheddar cheese
  • .5 cup of Sour cream, for serving
  • .5 cup of guacamole, for serving
  • .25 cup of Cilantro, for serving

Steps

  1. When ready to cook, set Smoker temperature 375℉ and preheat, lid closed for 15 minutes.
  2. On a large tray, spread out the tortilla chips evenly. First, sprinkle the salsa on chips, then the Kielbasa sausage, chicken and tri-tip. Top nachos with scallions, jalapeños, olives and lastly the cheese.
  3. Place tray on the grill and bake for approximately 10 to 15 minutes, or until cheese melts and the nachos are heated through.
  4. Serve with sour cream, guacamole and cilantro. Enjoy!
